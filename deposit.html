How to Deposit Money via M-PESA

  1. Go to the M-PESA menu on your phone.
  2. Select "Lipa na M-PESA".
  3. Select "Buy Goods and Services".
  4. Enter the till number 12333.
  5. Enter the amount you wish to deposit.
  6. Enter your M-PESA PIN and confirm the transaction.
  7. Wait for the confirmation SMS from M-PESA.
